About Our Work
We are passionate Kumbhar artisans creating handmade Ganpati idols, traditional clay and plaster pottery, along with small Maharashtrian festival items like oxen (Bail), forts, and Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj idols made with maval. Rooted in culture and devotion, our work blends heritage craftsmanship with eco-friendly practices. Every piece is shaped with care, faith, and love.
