About Our Work
We are passionate Kumbhar artisans creating handmade Ganpati idols and traditional clay pottery. Rooted in culture and devotion, our work blends heritage craftsmanship with eco-friendly practices. Every piece is shaped with care, faith, and love.

Our Heritage

We come from a traditional Kumbhaar family with generations of experience in clay craftsmanship. Our art is not just work—it is devotion passed down through time, shaped by hands that respect tradition and faith.

Handcrafted with Devotion

Every Ganpati idol is carefully handcrafted using natural clay and traditional techniques. From shaping to detailing, each step is done with patience, purity, and devotion to Lord Ganesha.

Custom Designs & Sizes

From small home idols to large festival murtis, we create custom sizes, colors, and designs based on your preference—while maintaining traditional beauty and grace.
